# Bank Empire v0.8.3 direction

V0.8.2 is the approved product and design foundation.

## Preserve

- Existing pages, progression, products, branches, competition, workforce, risk, reports, holdings and founder systems.
- Existing visual identity and overall navigation.
- Browser-first React/TypeScript/Vite architecture and local saves.

## Improve incrementally

- Reduce visual clutter without removing useful systems.
- Raise the readability floor and keep important information visually dominant.
- Delegate routine branch, credit, people and operating work to managers.
- Keep the player focused on strategy, expansion, leadership, capital and major exceptions.
- Rebalance branch profitability and marketing so growth is understandable and gradual.
- Add international expansion later as an extension of the existing game, not as a replacement.

## Guardrail

No full UI rewrite, no replacement game loop and no removal of major v0.8.2 systems without explicit approval.
